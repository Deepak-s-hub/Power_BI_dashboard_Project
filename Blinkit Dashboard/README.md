**1. Overview and Purpose of the sales dashboard**

An interactive analytical interface designed to visualize and monitor sales performance across different sections - 
such as time, outlets, product type, location, customer behaviour. It helps measure trends, performance, and making informed decisions.




**2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**

Shown as cards or scorecards at the top:

Metric:-

**Total Sales** = Total revenue generated across all outlets of Blinkit platform , indicating business scale and growth. 

**Average Sales** = Average revenue per sale or per outlet shows understanding customer purchasing behaviour. 

**Number of Items Sold** = Total number of products sold, reflects product movement by Volume and necessity. 

**Average Customer Rating** = Reflects Customer satisfaction and products quality, across customer reviews, measured out of 5.
